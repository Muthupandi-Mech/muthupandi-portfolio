Patent Highlight

Patent / Innovation

Self-Adjusting Bike Stand

The Self-Adjusting Bike Stand is a mechanical patent design that provides automatic stability on uneven surfaces using a pivot joint and torsion spring mechanism.

When deployed, the stand automatically adjusts to ground variations through pivot motion, while the torsion spring generates restoring torque to maintain balance under load.

This ensures stable support even on slopes or soft terrain such as sand or gravel. The system operates purely on mechanical principles without electronic components, making it a cost-effective, reliable, and maintenance-free solution.
